\section*{Project Narrative}

A persistent debate surrounds the assembly mechanisms shaping the human gut microbiota, with considerations of the niche paradigm versus priority effects significantly influencing optimal clinical treatment practices.
The objective of this proposed study is to develop a new statistical method capable of discerning between these distinct assembly processes, aiming to unveil deep insights into assembly mechanisms within unmanipulated gut systems.
Successful completion of this project will furnish a crucial statistical resource, offering the ability to identify assembly rules in the human gut microbiota and beyond.
